Steadfast Worldwide, Inc.

We dedicate ourselves to our customers, working closely with them during the design, sourcing, development, and manufacturing process to ensure we meet their design requirements while maintaining a competitive price all at a high-quality standard.

Our Mission

With over 25 years of experience in development, sourcing, manufacturing, and logistics we at Steadfast Worldwide, Inc. offer a vast amount of experience to support the needs of our customers and help to bring any project from conception to reality in a cost-effective and expedited manner.

About Us

Steadfast Worldwide, Inc. is a California based apparel and product manufacturer specializing in sourcing, development, production, and logistics for a diverse portfolio of apparel retailers and brands. We have offices and distribution centers in California, China, Mexico, and Peru.

Working with companies of all sizes ranging from start-ups to large brands and retailers, Steadfast Worldwide Inc. offers many services to manage the supply chain for customers, allowing them to focus on building and growing their brand.

We have a highly experienced staff in Asia, Latin America, and the USA with extensive experience in manufacturing to add the support needed in multiple regions. In addition, our Southern California office gives our partners in-person and hands-on support, allowing us to act fast with the development process and providing direct communication and reliable customer support in person.

Steadfast Worldwide Inc. is committed to working closely with our customers and supporting them in any way necessary to deliver first-quality products on time and at a competitive cost.

What We Do

We work hand in hand with our customers to take on any essential role for them, whether it be sourcing, development, production, quality, logistics, or the entire product lifecycle from beginning to end. We are there to support our customers. From start-ups to large companies, we take on the roles necessary for our customers to be successful, allowing them to spend more time focusing and strategizing their business and less time worrying about the development cycle.

FACILITIES

We have development and production offices in Ningbo China, Mexico City & Puebla Mexico, Lima Peru and Southern California, USA to support customers with sourcing, development, product management, quality assurance and on-time delivery of production orders.

SERVICES

We work within any scope necessary to support the growth of ourcustomers/partners. Steadfast Worldwide, Inc. can take your design conception to deliver final products to your door. We provide solutions for Development, Sourcing, Technical Design, Production, Quality, and Logistics.

PRODUCTS

Our product scope is vast, with our presence in Asia and Latin America. We produce apparel knit garments, woven garments, jackets, and performance apparel. We also manufacture accessories such as backpacks, socks, ceramic products, Christmas ornaments, footwear, home goods, and other items. Finally, we work with other consumer goods such as wood furniture, woodtoys, plushy stuffed characters, and electronics such as massagers and drones.

QUALITY

Our mantra is quality. We want your customers to be return customers. We understand that your repeat success is the most important thing. We offer in-house quality audits for inline and final inspections. We also work with outside partners to provide third-party inspections and testing on products and materials.

COMPLIANCE AND REGULATORY STANDARDS

Our mission is to ensure product meets industry and regulatory standards. All materials we purchase are sourced responsibly. Were partnered with QIMA a well known 3rd party agency to ensure we deliver on our promise of Products that meet all required standards. Test reports are made available prior to shipping.

LOGITICS AND Delivery

Our USA office gives Steadfast Worldwide Inc., the ability to offer our customers DDP Terms (Delivered Duty Paid) where we manage the entire logistics process, including customs imports and delivering completed production directly to our customers warehouse anywhere in the world.
