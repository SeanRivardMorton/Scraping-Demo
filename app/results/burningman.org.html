From the Nevada Desert to the World: Lessons in Community Resilience

Meet three Burners who are putting their skills to work in Black Rock City and out in the world. “We go to Burning Man to prepare for the world. That's our one week where we get to build our resilience, build our capacity — emotional, spiritual, physical — to go back and serve in the world and be able to give back.” -Yasmine Elbaggari
